Olympia is an ancient Greek city located on the western side of the Peloponnese peninsula. The city's history spans over 1,000 years, with evidence of humans dating back to prehistoric times. Welcome on this site!

Welcome to the fascinating world of the Ancient Olympic Games, a monumental event that showcased the pinnacle of athletic prowess and cultural importance in ancient Greece. Held in honor of the mighty god Zeus, these games captivated the hearts and minds of thousands, bringing together a tapestry of city-states in the pursuit of athletic glory and spiritual growth. Journey with us as we explore the origins, the events, and the legends that shaped this extraordinary celebration, transcending time and leaving a lasting impact on sports, culture, and society.
